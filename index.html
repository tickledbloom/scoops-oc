About Scoops

You may have heard of or visited our Los Angeles locations. We're known for our unique variety of ice cream flavors, which range from green tea to popcorn, brown bread, balsamic cherry, coffee Guinness and goat cheese and lavender honey. Scoops OC opened up shop in July 2015, within the vibrant and upcoming community of downtown Santa Ana. We hope you'll stop by for a tasty treat!
